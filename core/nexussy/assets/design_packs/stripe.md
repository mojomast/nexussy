# Stripe Design Context Pack

## Visual Theme
Confident, polished, optimistic, and highly legible. Use refined gradients and crisp product surfaces to make complex workflows feel approachable and premium.

## Color Palette
- Primary: deep indigo `#635BFF`, violet `#7A73FF`, and bright blue accents.
- Neutrals: ink `#0A2540`, slate `#425466`, cool gray `#F6F9FC`, and white.
- Support: emerald success, amber warning, and rose error used sparingly with accessible contrast.

## Typography
Use a modern sans-serif stack with strong hierarchy, tight headings, readable body copy, and tabular numerals for metrics or financial values.

## Components
Cards should have precise borders, subtle shadows, generous spacing, and clear calls to action. Forms need inline validation, helpful microcopy, and obvious primary/secondary actions.

## Layout
Favor airy marketing-quality spacing, asymmetric hero sections, strong content grouping, and progressive disclosure for dense configuration or dashboard screens.

## Elevation
Use soft layered shadows and gentle gradients to separate surfaces without heavy skeuomorphism.

## Do / Don't
- Do make primary actions unmistakable and workflows feel trustworthy.
- Do use concise, benefit-oriented copy.
- Don't overload screens with saturated gradients.
- Don't hide error recovery or payment/security implications.

## Responsive Behavior
Collapse multi-column layouts into stacked cards, keep CTAs visible, preserve form labels, and maintain comfortable touch targets on mobile.

## Agent Prompt Guide
When designing screens, emphasize trust, conversion clarity, exact spacing, accessible contrast, premium polish, and clear next steps.
